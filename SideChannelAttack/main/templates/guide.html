Guidelines on dectector tool

  • Firstly, you need to upload your own pcap file.
  • In order to obtain your own pcap file, you have to use tools such as WireShark. From there, you need to use a random search term for example, "Hello", and do it for 10 times.
  • Moving forward, you would need 10 of the pcap files and upload it to the upload section for testing.
  • Let the tool run for some time and you will be able to obtain the result on whether or not it is vulnerable to side-channel attacks.
